Route-Note: A Journey Worth Mapping Route-Note is more than a travel journal — it's a personal archive of adventures. Every destination becomes a note in the melody of your travels, each pin on the map a heartbeat frozen in time. With intuitive features to mark locations, upload photos, and write about your experiences, this platform transforms memories into a visual story. Whether you’re wandering sunlit coastlines, trekking mountain trails, or rediscovering your hometown, Route-Note helps you trace your path and relive every step. Let your map grow with you — one journey, one story, one memory at a time.

About Us

We’re Subham, Sumant, and Udipta — 6th-semester Computer Science students from Girijananda Chowdhury University. We built Route-Note thinking it’d be easier than exams. (Spoiler: it wasn’t.)

Like any curious explorer, we wanted more than just photos — we wanted to capture places, feelings, and stories. Route-Note became our way to map memories so they don't fade (unless we crash the database again).

Whether you're off on an epic adventure or a casual stroll, Route-Note lets you pin locations, save memories, and relive your journey, one route at a time — built by travelers (who sometimes forget to travel) for travelers, from three students learning, coding, and surviving college one bug at a time.
